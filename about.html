About SINFIG

Learn how to use our Sinhala text tools

Text Converter

The Text Converter allows you to convert Sinhala text between different formats in real-time. Perfect for converting legacy fonts to Unicode or typing in Singlish.

1

Enter Your Text

Type or paste your text in the left input area. The text can be in Unicode, Legacy format, or Singlish.

2

Select Conversion Type

Choose from 12 conversion types including Unicode to Legacy, Legacy to Unicode, or Singlish to Unicode.

3

Get Instant Results

Your converted text appears on the right side in real-time. Click the copy button to use it anywhere.

Real-time conversion as you type
12 different conversion types
One-click copy to clipboard
Works on all devices

Font Generator

Create beautiful Sinhala text images with custom fonts, colors, and styles. Perfect for social media posts, designs, and graphics.

1

Enter Your Text

Type your text in the input area. Use the type toggle (1-4) to select text processing method.

2

Choose Font & Styling

Select from multiple Sinhala fonts, adjust size, color, text style (bold, italic, underline), and alignment.

3

Generate & Download

Click "Generate Image" to create your custom text image. Preview it and download for your use.

Multiple Sinhala font options
Customizable size and color
Text styling (bold, italic, underline)
Download as PNG image
